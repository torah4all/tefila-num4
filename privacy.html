Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Eli's Prayers ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use our web application.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or process any personal data from our users. The App is designed to maximize your privacy.

Local Storage

We use your browser's Local Storage to save your preferences, such as:

  • Theme preference (Dark/Light mode)
  • Font size settings
  • Prayer text alignment preferences

This data remains strictly on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third parties.

3. Location Data

If you use the Zmanim (Halachic Times) feature, your device may request access to your location. This is used solely to calculate accurate prayer times for your specific location. Location data is processed locally or sent directly to the Zmanim API provider (MyZmanim) solely for the purpose of retrieving times, and is not stored by us.

4. Third-Party Services

We use reputable third-party services for specific features:

  • MyZmanim: For Halachic times.
  • Fouita: For the weather widget.

These services may collect anonymous usage data subject to their own privacy policies.

By using this App, you agree to these terms. content is provided "as is" for religious and educational purposes.
